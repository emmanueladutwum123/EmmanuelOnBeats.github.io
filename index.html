About Me

Rhythms and Strings: A Versatile Music & Arts Portfolio

My journey in music began in childhood, inspired by my mother, who played the brass band as a drummer in the Presbyterian Church of Ghana, Abeka Congregation (now Royal Priesthood Congregation). My mother introduced me to a talented drummer at the church, where I laid my musical foundation.

Starting as a gospel drummer, I performed in numerous churches in Ghana, including renowned congregations such as Presbyterian, Pentecost, ICGS, and Family Life International. In the United States, I have played for the Pentecost Church in Cleveland, Ohio, Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, Columbia, New York, and Orange County, California.

Over the past 12 years, my passion for the drum set has flourished, complemented by my skills in guitar (4 years) and piano (5 years). My musical repertoire spans diverse genres, including Jazz, Gospel, Hillsong, Reggae, Highlife, Afrobeat, Pop, Hip-Hop, R&B, Hiplife, and more.

At Soka University, I have taken advanced Jazz Ensemble and Strings classes, further refining my craft. I am recognized as the energetic and skillful drummer on campus, a title that reflects my dedication to the art of percussion and my passion for musical expression.
